<div class="card">
  <h2>Planner Logic</h2>
  <p>
    This planner is designed as a progressive content system, not a collection of isolated posts.
  </p>
  <p>
    Each week reduces friction, builds trust, and increases audience readiness for action. Lower-friction asks appear first. More direct action prompts appear only after enough context, clarity, and credibility have been established.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="card">
  <h2>Content Principles</h2>
  <p><strong>Clarity over jargon:</strong> Content should reduce friction, not perform expertise for its own sake.</p>
  <p><strong>Human tone over institutional tone:</strong> Readers should feel guided, not processed.</p>
  <p><strong>Visual simplicity over clutter:</strong> Every asset should have one clear communication job.</p>
  <p><strong>Educational value over filler:</strong> Every post should either inform, reassure, or move the audience forward.</p>
</div>
